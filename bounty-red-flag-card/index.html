Check Fact to resolve Evidence state
Funding evidence
01

Payout is not verifiably funded. Blocking

Can authoritative evidence show that the stated payout is funded and available?

Availability evidence
02

The claim window is closed, expired, or ambiguous. Blocking

Is the claim window open now, with the deadline and eligibility rules confirmed?

Competition evidence
03

Someone else is assigned or already building.

Have assignees, claims, pull requests, forks, comments, and submissions been checked?

Acceptance evidence
04

Acceptance criteria are missing or subjective.

Are the deliverable and acceptance tests objective enough to verify before work starts?

Authority evidence
05

The person offering payment may lack acceptance authority.

Is it clear who can accept delivery, resolve disputes, and release the payout?

Upfront cost evidence
06

You must pay, bond, purchase, or provision first. Blocking

Can the work be attempted without an unjustified bond, purchase, subscription, or spend?

Access evidence
07

Delivery requires private credentials or production access. Blocking

Can the deliverable be completed without secrets, customer data, or live-system access?

Third-party dependency evidence
08

Payment depends on third-party adoption, promotion, or approval.

Is acceptance controlled by the named payer rather than an unrelated third party?

Scope evidence
09

The real scope exceeds the headline task.

Does the estimate include setup, tests, docs, review cycles, support, and collection?

Project baseline evidence
10

The project baseline is unhealthy or inactive.

Does the baseline build, test, and provide enough maintainer activity for acceptance?

Rights and compliance evidence
11

Rights or compliance obligations are unclear.

Are licenses, data rights, disclosure duties, taxes, and platform rules understood?

Economics evidence
12

Expected hourly return falls below your floor.

After costs and payment risk, does expected return still clear the required hourly floor?

Decision rule

Hard stops outrank the count.

Unfunded or unavailable work, unauthorized access, prohibited conduct, and unjustified mandatory spending stay blocked until resolved.

Economics check

Price the whole path to payment.

Include review, revisions, communication, costs, and collection risk.

(payout × payment probability − unavoidable costs) ÷ total hours
