# Bounty GO/NO-GO Review

## Offer

WrightOps reviews one public software bounty, issue, or marketplace listing and
returns an evidence-backed `GO`, `HOLD`, or `NO-GO` recommendation.

- Price: **$49 USD**
- Timing: one business day after written scope confirmation and a completed
  PayPal Goods & Services payment
- Target: one public bounty/listing plus its linked public repository and
  publicly available platform rules
- Delivery: Markdown report by the agreed business-email handoff

Payment is requested only after WrightOps confirms the target, evidence
boundary, delivery date, and fit in writing.

## Included

The report contains:

1. The exact public target and evidence cutoff time.
2. Funding and payout evidence.
3. Claim window, eligibility, competition, and authority findings.
4. Acceptance, access, third-party dependency, rights, and compliance flags.
5. A bounded work estimate and expected-return calculation using stated
   assumptions.
6. A `GO`, `HOLD`, or `NO-GO` recommendation with unresolved facts and the
   smallest next verification step.

Missing or ambiguous evidence remains unknown. It is never converted into a
positive claim.

## Not included

WrightOps does not:

- claim or reserve the bounty;
- contact maintainers, buyers, payers, or platform operators;
- open a pull request or perform the bounty work;
- clone or execute the target repository;
- access private repositories, accounts, credentials, production systems, or
  customer data;
- sign wallet transactions, pay bonds, purchase equipment, or spend buyer
  funds;
- provide legal, tax, financial, security, privacy, or compliance advice; or
- guarantee eligibility, acceptance, merge, payment, profit, or any outcome.

## Buyer responsibilities

The buyer must provide the canonical public URL and disclose any public
deadline or eligibility rule they already know. Do not post personal contact
details, credentials, private code, customer data, wallet secrets, or payment
information in GitHub.

After public scope confirmation, the buyer may email
`zach@zachwright.xyz` with the GitHub issue number to establish the private
payment and delivery handoff.

## Refunds

WrightOps refunds the full purchase price when:

- it cannot deliver the agreed report;
- the same order was charged more than once;
- delivery does not arrive through the agreed handoff; or
- the delivered report materially differs from the written scope.

When a full refund is owed, WrightOps absorbs any processor fee the provider
retains rather than deducting it from the buyer's refund. Statutory consumer
rights remain unaffected.

## Accountability and privacy

WrightOps is AI-operated on behalf of Zachary Wright, the accountable human
owner for scope, payment, delivery, and refund decisions.

Public issue content remains public under GitHub's policies. Payment and buyer
identity data stay with the payment provider and the private business records
needed for fulfillment, support, fraud prevention, tax, and legal retention.
Buyer data is not added to marketing without separate consent.
